About Me

It started on a normal day like any other. Adam was enjoying his days as a Plumbing and Mechanical Engineer - designing HVAC and plumbing systems for commercial buildings. When not working, he would regularly spend his free time playing video games or camping/hiking if the weather was nice. But suddenly, everything changed...

Adam was bit by the coding bug! He was consumed by the need to learn web development and began making new applications in earnest. Rather than let his new found powers go to waste, he donned the mantle of Coderman and started his journey into the world of Full Stack development.
